### Complete Cover-Up Toolkit

The Complete Cover-Up Toolkit is a digital workflow product for working tattoo artists. It is not positioned as beginner tattoo instruction and does not claim to make every tattoo coverable. Its purpose is to help artists assess cover-up projects, manage client expectations, price risk, identify laser-first situations, and know when to modify, refer, or decline a project.

## Workflow

The landing page describes the toolkit workflow as:

1. Consult: understand the client, old tattoo, skin, expectations, and constraints.
2. Qualify: decide whether the project should proceed, change, require laser, or stop.
3. Plan: document the recommendation in a client-facing project plan.
4. Price: quote around planning, complexity, session load, and risk.
5. Design: map coverage logic before spending time on final art.

## Audience

Good-fit buyers include working tattoo artists with cover-up demand, artists who underquote because cover-ups feel uncertain, and artists who want better consultations, cleaner plans, and premium bookings.

The toolkit is not positioned for total beginners, artists looking for one trick that covers every tattoo, or artists unwilling to say no when the project is wrong.
